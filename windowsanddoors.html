SLIDING

The syetem is with horizontal opening frames.Modern windows are usually glazed (DGU) or covered in some other transparent or translucent material.

PIVOTING

The system opens vertically with right, left or center pivot.

GUILLOTINE

The syetem is with two vertically sliding frames.

TURNABLE CORNER

The solution optimize space allowing more space for passageway.

Awning

Awning are hinged on the top and open outward from the bottom, allowing for ventilation and protection from the rain.

Casement

A casement is a window/door that is attached to its frame by one or more hinges at the side. They are used singly or in pairs within a common frame, in which case they are hinged on the outside. Casement windows are often held open using a casement stay.

Double Hung

Double-Hung have two operating sash that move up and down allowing for ventilation on the top, bottom or both.

Bifold

Bifold systems allow for any living or working area to be expanded to include an unobstructed view of the outdoors.

Louvre

Louvre windows/door is a window/door blind or shutter with horizontal slats that are angled to admit light and air, but to keep out rain and direct sunshine. The angle of the slats may be adjustable, usually in blinds and windows, or fixed.

Anodized or Powder Coating

Powder coating: economical, longest lasting, and most color-durable quality finishes available on virtually any type of metal
Anodized coating: A fairly simple process that's used to protect an aluminum surface from undue damage and wear, anodizing aluminum adds a protective coating of aluminum oxide to the metal itself
